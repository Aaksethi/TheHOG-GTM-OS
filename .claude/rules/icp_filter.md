# Rule — ICP Filter

No contact enters any workflow without passing the company
ICP filter in Skill 01 first.

Minimum requirements for a contact to be processed (all criteria
are defined in BRAND.md — this rule enforces that they are met):
- Company matches an ICP segment/category defined in BRAND.md
- Company has the channel/market presence required by BRAND.md
- Company revenue falls within the band defined in BRAND.md
- Contact title matches one of the buyer personas defined in BRAND.md

Any contact that cannot be confirmed against these criteria
is marked Needs Review and held for operator decision.
Zero exceptions for volume — quality over quantity always.
